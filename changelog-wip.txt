BotC v1.4.6 - Feb 3, 2026
Changes and features:
- The modpack now includes a homemade map!
- Added custom banners for the new map.
- Added new menu backgrounds reflecting the new map.
- Added a particle to show who's on the block.
- Added a link to the modpack Discord on the main menu.
- The Singleplayer menu can now be accessed by clicking on the Blood on the Clocktower logo while holding Left Shift.
- Changed house/player colors to a more colorblind-friendly set.
- Ghost vote particles now play at all times during dusk instead of just during votes.
- Removed Clean F3 as it is not being updated to modern versions.

Fixed bugs:
- Changelog cannot be scrolled.
- "Mark for execution" displays for all players, not just Storytellers.
- Singleplayer world list does not have a custom background.
- Singleplayer loading screen does not have a custom background.
